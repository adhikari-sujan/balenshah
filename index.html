Who is Balen

Balendra Shah (born 27 April 1990), popularly known as Balen, is a Nepalese politician, structural engineer, and rapper who is the prime minister of Nepal since 2026. He became leader after the RSP party won a big victory in the 2026 Nepal election. He previously served as the 15th mayor of Kathmandu from 2022 to 2026, the first independent candidate to hold the office. At age 35, he is the world's youngest serving state leader.
